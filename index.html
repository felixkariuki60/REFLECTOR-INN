Tucked Away in Kamulu

OUR
STORY

Off Kangundo Road, near Kamulu Police Station, you'll find a place that Nairobi keeps returning to. Reflector Inn was built with one belief: that rest, good food, and community belong in the same space.

An open field. A water fountain. A bar that stays open all night. Rooms that feel like home. A gym with specialised trainers who actually know your name. A sauna. A kinyozi. A carwash.

"Local pub but with good ambience." — Samuel M. Mbindyo, Local Guide

Open 24 hours. Ksh 500–2,500 per person. Always ready for you.
